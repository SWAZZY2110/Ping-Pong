Pong





Instructions

Instructions for the game itself:
1.Mouse movement moves the player paddle
2.The ball will first move towards the computer's paddle
3.The computer's paddle will hit the ball towards the user's half of the form
4.If the player manages to hit the ball with their paddle, the ball will bounce back towards the computer's half of the form
5.If the player misses the ball then they have lost and can either exit the game with the X button or restart the game with the R button
